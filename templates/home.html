Pneumonia

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs ( Refer to Figure 1a ). The air sacs may fill with fluid or pus (purulent material), causing cough with phlegm or pus, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing. It could also be a serious complication of COVID-19.

Symptoms
  • Cough, which may produce greenish, yellow or even bloody mucus.
  • Fever, sweating and shaking chills.
  • Shortness of breath.
  • Rapid, shallow breathing.
