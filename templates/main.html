About

This is a Pneumonia Predictor that is based on Deep Learning. Its a project made by me (Rajat Gupta) for Future Ready Talent Internship. Just navigate to Predictor menu on the navbar and upload a picture of a chest X-Ray, that you want to check. It detects Pneumonia with 95% accuracy.
